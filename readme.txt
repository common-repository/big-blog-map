== Description ==

The Big Blog Map wordpress plugin allows you to Geotag posts so that they appear on a Google map. The Big Blog Map is great for

- Travel Bloggers - Share your route with friends and family
- City Bloggers - Map your favourite cafes, restaurants and bars
- Pirates - Write blogs about where you store your treasure

== Installation ==
1) Install and activate
2) Go to 'Pages' and click on the newly created 'Big Blog Map' Page
3) Check out your map and cutstomise it
4) Geotag your posts by editing them and clicking on the map beneath the content area

IMPORTANT: The map will not work properly until at least 2 posts are tagged
